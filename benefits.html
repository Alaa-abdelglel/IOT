What are the benefits of the Internet of Things?

What we have seen so far is just the beginning of IoT. It is an ever growing technology which will show its impact in a decade. Internet of Things can be used for a wide range of industries as it brings customer satisfaction into consideration, which is an ultimate goal for every business. It is a technology which will undoubtedly make the world a better place to live in. Also, there is a need to encrypt the data and to protect it as there are billions of devices connected via IoT. The chances of breaching its security are continually increasing, which is an area of concern and should be taken care of.
